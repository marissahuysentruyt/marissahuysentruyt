blog

I am not a writer. But, with the encouragement of some developer friends that I look up to and have so much respect for (both of these guys are top-notch, supportive, PLUS they are so willing to share and help me learn!), I started a blog. As much as I don't think my story is all that interesting, maybe others will think so. I can't be the only one out there struggling to teach myself, so maybe I can shed some light on how I've been doing things and learning front end. I have no right answers, just the answers that have worked for me.

I'm not pleading with anybody to read these. I'm not making any money off of these posts. But by writing them, I feel like I'm holding myself more accountable. Each blog is helping me view myself as the developer I want to be.
